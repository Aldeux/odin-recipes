Tortas fritas

Description

This delicious dough is a traditional "dish" in south america. It is accompanied with tea, coffee, mate, any beverage you would like. Mostly, people make them on rainy days.

Ingredients

  • salt
  • flour
  • cooking oil
  • necessary water

Steps

  1. Mix the flour,salt, cooking oil.
  2. Add necessary water until the dough becomes together.
  3. Kned it until smooth, cover it and let it repose for a few minutes
  4. Make little balls from it and flat them with a wooden roller.
  5. Have a plate with some paper cooking sheets to absord oil later.
  6. Put a recipient with cooking oil (just the amount needed to cover the doughs) on the stove until it reaches 180 degrees.
  7. When the oil reaches that temperature, put a flat dough, cook it on one side and the other too until it becomes a bit brown.
  8. Remove it and place another dough in the hot oil.
  9. Repeat until done.
