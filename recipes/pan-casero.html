Pan casero

Description

Homemade bread which goes with anything. A sandwich, peanut butter, jam, etc.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • salt
  • cooking oil
  • water
  • yeast
  • sugar

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, salt and make a hole in the middle.
  2. Add the yeast with some sugar and add warm water and mix until smooth.
  3. Add neccesary water until the dough becomes together.
  4. Add oil in this stage and start kneading.
  5. When smooth, let it rest until it doubles its size.
  6. After it has arisen, threw it to the oven at 180 degrees.
  7. To check if the bread is done, punch it with a knife, if it exits without residues, then it is done.
