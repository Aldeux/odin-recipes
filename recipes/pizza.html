Pizza

Description

Homemade delicious pizza, to share with your closer loved ones.

Ingredients

  • salt
  • flour
  • water
  • yeast
  • sugar
  • cheese
  • cooking oil
  • tomato sauce
  • spices of choice

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, salt and make a hole in the middle.
  2. Add the yeast with some sugar and add warm water and mix until smooth.
  3. Add neccesary water until the dough becomes together.
  4. Add oil in this stage and start kneading.
  5. When smooth, let it rest until it doubles its size.
  6. After it has arisen, threw it to your kitchen top, which needs to have flour on it.
  7. Make it as flat as possible, dont press it or use a wooden roller since you'll lose the bubbles inside the dough. The edges needs to be wider than the rest of the pizza to have a crust.
  8. When you are satisfied with the look of your pizza, threw it on a pizza pan and then into the oven at 180 degrees.
  9. When the bottom of the pizza starts to get a bit brown, cover the top with tomato sauce.
  10. When the bottom becomes brown, it's time to threw the cheese on top, you can turn off your oven or put it at minimum.
  11. When the cheese is melted, then the pizza is done to be served.
